Soonlist's work will be done when:

- Someone anywhere from Walla Walla, Portland, Detroit, Queens, to Koenji can leave Instagram and not only stay connected to community events across their interests, but show up more to:
  - Listening deeply at experimental music shows on a weeknight
  - Reading at the poetry open-mic at their local bookstore
  - And finally attending the organizing meeting of that activist org they've been interested in for years
- AND the organizers in each of those thriving scenes can pour more energy into what they love:
  - Building friendships, cross-pollinating with other scenes, and taking time offline to relax
  - While they're confident their event information is reaching both their core community and newcomers
  - And their scene's continuing flourishing isn't dependent on any attention-economy platform

Until then, there's lots of work to do!

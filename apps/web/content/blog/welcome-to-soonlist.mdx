We're thrilled to announce the launch of the Soonlist blog. This is where we'll share product updates, behind-the-scenes stories, and tips to help you get the most out of Soonlist.

## What is Soonlist?

Soonlist helps you save events by parsing them with AI. Whether you spot an event on social media, get a text from a friend, or see a flyer on the street — just capture it and Soonlist takes care of the rest.

## What to expect

Here's what you can look forward to on this blog:

- **Product updates** — New features, improvements, and what we're working on next
- **Tips and tricks** — Get the most out of Soonlist with helpful guides
- **Community stories** — How people are using Soonlist to stay on top of their events
- **Engineering deep dives** — A look under the hood at how we build Soonlist
